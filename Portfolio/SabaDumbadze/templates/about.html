ვინ ვარ მე?

Even though I am a teenager, I take my work seriously. I spend a lot of my time learning new ways to make websites faster and easier to use. I don't just want a site to look good; I want it to work perfectly for the person using it. For me, coding is about solving problems and making ideas real.

My Work Style

I prefer to keep things simple. Whether I am working on the backend with Django or styling a page with CSS, I try to keep my code organized so it’s easy to manage. I believe that a good website doesn't need to be complicated to be effective. I focus on clean layouts and making sure everything is responsive on mobile devices.

Skills & Learning

I have experience with Python, Django, and the basic building blocks of the web like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am also comfortable using Git for version control and Figma for planning out designs. I am always looking for new technologies to learn because the web is constantly changing, and I want to keep up with it.

What I aim for

My goal is to help people and small businesses get a professional online presence. I enjoy collaborating on projects where I can learn something new and deliver a final product that someone is proud to show off. If you have an idea for a project, I’d love to help you build it.
